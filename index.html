How to play

  1. One player creates a room and shares the six-character code or invite link.
  2. Each commander privately places five ships on a 10 × 10 grid.
  3. Take turns firing at one coordinate. Red means hit; white means miss.
  4. Sink all five enemy ships to win.

A hit does not grant an extra turn. Boards are committed before battle and can be checked when the game ends.
